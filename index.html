ABOUT ME


TL;DR ~ tech enthusiast fascinated by the intersections between hardware, software, and digital media & aspiring to create with the aim to stimulate social growth.

This past year, I've been involved as an organizer for hackathons like Bitcamp and Technica, an advocate for the menstrual movement by founding the PERIOD. chapter in College Park, and a research fellow at the Wireless Information Network Lab (WINLAB) at Rutgers University to improve predictive IoT behaviors with machine learning.
